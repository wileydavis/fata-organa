They thought it was a weapon. But it's a gift.

INTERCEPTED — EMERGENCY FREQUENCY 7.83 MHz

This is Marcus Reed, broadcasting on emergency frequency from somewhere off the coast of Baja. I don't expect anyone's listening.

Three weeks ago I stole a boat in Quito and pointed it north. Toward a place the rest of the world has given up on. Toward something I can't explain — a dream, recurring, a woman standing at the edge of the land where it drops into the Pacific. I don't know who she is. I don't know what she wants. But I know she's real, and I know she's inside.

No one goes back into the zone. I'm going back into the zone.

These transmissions are my record. If you're hearing this, keep listening. It matters that someone knows.
